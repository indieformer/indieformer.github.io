About the game

Feed the Scorchpot is a tabletop roguelike where you manage a medieval farm, cook meals, and try to satisfy a dragon who has very strong opinions about food — and very few qualms about burning things.

Every run is different. Every roll matters. And every dish is one bad decision away from disaster.

What it is

Clever one second.
Panicked the next.

A game that rewards smart thinking and punishes one bad roll in equal measure.

🎲 Dice-driven roguelike Every harvest, every recipe, every catastrophe rolls from the dice pool. No two runs play the same way.
🐉 A very hungry dragon Your landlord, your critic, your nemesis. Keep the Scorchpot fed — or face the consequences.
🍳 Tabletop feel, digital play Built like a board game. Plays like a roguelike. Feels like both at once — in the best possible way.
🌾 Build, harvest, cook, survive Manage your farm board, chain combos, discover recipes, and try to make it through 20 years without going bankrupt.
