The premise

You're a broke gladiator, and every fight starts at the slot machine. Spin to load your gear, then roll a d20 and let it play out. Between fights, you build the run: heal up at the Sanitarium, buy new gear from the Merchant, and stack your deck for what's next. Win enough to buy your freedom. Or go out swinging.

how it plays

It's a card game that runs on a slot machine.

Your items are the deck; the machine decides what you play with. Every reroll costs more than the last. This is both a slots and combat game at the same time.
