Early Access Highlights

Here are 10 of the 18 new Early Access additions to Waypoint.

VEIN

Die For The Lich

A dice-driven deck-builder where luck meets strategy in a chaotic battle against folklore monsters. Roll enchanted dice, forge powerful gear, and shape your fate one toss at a time as you push toward the ultimate showdown with the Lich.

$9.99 | PC

Into The Grid

A roguelike deckbuilder reimagined as a tactical board game — where every card fuels both your attack and your next move. Manage resources, chain powerful Commands, and plan every step across a high-stakes digital landscape pulsing with risk and reward.

$14.99 | PC, Mac, Linux

Tavern Keeper

A whimsical tavern sim where chaos, charm, and storytelling collide. Build and run your dream inn — hiring staff, decorating every nook, and keeping rowdy patrons (and fires) under control.

$29.99 | PC

Terra Firma 2

Shape continents, split valleys, and raise mountain ranges with tectonic power, then watch life emerge across forests, deserts, and tundras. This next-gen world simulator lets you sculpt terrain, control climate, and witness ecosystems adapt in real time—down to the flow of rivers and the movement of wildlife.

$19.99 | PC

Hoop Land

A retro-styled basketball sim where every possession feels alive thanks to true 3D rim and ball physics. Run games, spectate, or sim entire seasons as college and pro leagues blend into one endlessly replayable sandbox.

$14.99 | PC, Mac

VEIN

Survive the collapse in a living, breathing apocalypse. Scavenge, build, and fight to endure a world where everything—and everyone—is out to get you.

$19.99 | PC, Linux

Super Fantasy Kingdom

Rebuild, defend, and rise again in an endless cycle of ruin and rebirth. Each night brings new waves of monsters to test your walls and your wits, while each dawn offers a chance to rebuild, recruit, and refine your strategy.

$14.99 | PC

Species: Unknown

Board an abandoned spaceship, complete high-stakes contracts, and try to survive what’s lurking in the dark. Play solo or with up to four mercenaries as you investigate derelict vessels, identify unpredictable threats, and fight—or flee—for your life.

$9.99 | PC

SWAPMEAT

Steal limbs, swap powers, and clean up Carl’s mess in this chaotic co-op roguelite shooter. As the newest SmartMeat™ scientist, you’ll drop onto mutant-infested worlds, rip enemies apart for triple-jump legs or turret-dropping torsos, and stitch yourself into the ultimate problem-solver.

$24.99 | PC, Mac

Sunken Engine

Inherit your late father’s ship repair business in a quiet coastal town where the sea hides more than wrecks. Each vessel you fix carries whispers of something ancient and wrong beneath the waves.

$9.99 | PC
