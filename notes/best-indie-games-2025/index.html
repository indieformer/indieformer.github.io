2025's Best Indie Games

A curated sweep of 2025’s releases, pulled from across the year and gathered in one place for anyone still looking for something worth playing.

The Best of 2025

In the previous issue, we dug deep for the hidden gems you might have missed. This time, we’re looking at the ones you (probably) didn’t.

But this isn’t just a copy-paste of the year's best-sellers.

This is a collection of the games that defined 2025 for us. From the long-awaited sequels that actually delivered, to the indie darlings that punched way above their weight class, these are the titles that dominated the conversation and set new standards for their genres.

These aren't just the games everyone played; they’re the ones everyone should play.

The Tacticians: Puzzles & Logic

Games where the world itself is a puzzle to be untangled.

Blue Prince

This ingenious "blueprint roguelike" demands you draft your own path through a shifting manor, blending architectural strategy with deep, interconnected mysteries. It respects your intelligence with fiercely challenging puzzles that transform obsessive note-taking into a thrilling, addictive triumph.

$29.99 | PC, Mac, Xbox, PlayStation

Öoo

This textless masterclass reinvents platforming, challenging you to traverse a mysterious world solely through the explosive physics of a bomb-wielding caterpillar. Its dense, organic design creates a rewarding loop where deep mechanical complexity is taught silently through pure play.

$9.99 | PC

MotionRec

Traverse a devastated world by recording and replaying your own movements, turning empty air into essential footholds. This striking monochrome puzzler reinvents 2D traversal, blending fresh, brain-bending logic with undeniable charm.

$9.99 | PC

Is This Seat Taken?

Play the role of social architect in this delightfully cozy puzzler, harmonizing picky characters into perfect seating arrangements. It blends low-pressure logic with a heartwarming tale of self-discovery, proving that finding where everyone fits is a deeply satisfying art.

$9.99 | Pc, Mac, Linux, Switch, iOS, Android

The Adrenaline Rush: Action & Mastery

Games defined by rhythm, reflexes, and the joy of movement.

Mortal Sin

A visually arresting action-roguelike that looks like a comic book in motion. It focuses on heavy, visceral melee combat where you must tactically dismember grotesque foes, blending high-contrast horror with a deep, rewarding combo system.

$24.99 | PC

CRUEL

A punishing run-and-gun horror shooter set in a rusted industrial hellscape. It strips away the safety of modern shooters, demanding twitch reflexes and resource management in a gritty, low-poly world that feels actively hostile to your existence.

$9.99 | PC

Absolum

Fusing arcade beat 'em up energy with roguelite depth, this heartfelt adventure weaves a surprising tale of grief and rebellion. It demands strategic mastery of spells and combos, delivering a thunderous, hand-drawn experience backed by an all-star soundtrack.

$24.99 | PC, Switch, Playstation

FlyKnight

Visceral, limb-severing combat defines this bite-sized triumph, revitalizing the dungeon crawler by abandoning traditional leveling. It delivers a refreshingly classic, atmospheric adventure that boldly defies the modern Soulslike formula.

$5.99 | PC

The Dreamers: Atmosphere & Emotion

Games that prioritize mood, narrative weight, and emotional resonance.

Sword of the Sea

This atmospheric surfing adventure invites you to glide across shifting dunes and undulating seas on a "hoversword." It captures a meditative, rhythmic momentum, turning the landscape itself into a half-pipe for your exploration.

$29.99 | PC, Playstation

Skate Story

As a fragile demon made of glass and pain, you must kickflip through the Underworld to swallow the moon. This surreal, psychedelic fever dream blends grounded, ritualistic skating physics with a hypnotic audiovisual style, ensuring every victory is stylish and every failure is a shattering spectacle.

$19.99 | PC, Mac

DELTARUNE

This parallel adventure transcends nostalgia, delivering a heartwarming reunion wrapped in evolved, bullet-dodging combat and a genre-defining soundtrack. It strikes a perfect balance between immersion and challenge, offering a deeply affecting journey that feels less like a game and more like returning home.

$24.99 | PC, Switch, Playstation

CARIMARA: Beneath the forlorn limbs

As a mute sorcerer born of moss, you conjure inquiries from dust to unravel a house stitched from grief. This visually arresting vignette trades combat for the delicate art of asking, creating a haunting masterpiece of emotional resonance.

$4.99 | PC, Mac

DOGWALK

Cast as a lumbering dog guiding a child, you explore a stunning, handcrafted paper world in this zero-stress winter vignette. It serves as a heartwarming digital snow globe, prioritizing pure, cozy vibes and tangible artistry over challenge.

Free | PC, Mac, Linux

The Wildcards: Genre-Benders & Subversions

Games that subvert expectations and embrace the bizarre.

Promise Mascot Agency

Cast as an exiled Yakuza, you must manage living, unstable mascots in a cursed, conspiracy-ridden town. This genre-bending fever dream blends dark comedy with genuine heart, inviting you to drift a souped-up Kei truck through a surreal world that is as grotesque as it is joyful.

$24.99 | PC, Switch, Xbox, Playstation

Wanderstop

This deceptively cozy tea shop simulator forces a reluctant warrior into a ritual of patience, subverting genre expectations to explore the raw reality of burnout. It transforms mundane tasks into a deeply therapeutic narrative that lands with unexpected emotional weight.

$24.99 | PC, Xbox, PlayStation
