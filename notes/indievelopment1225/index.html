Early Access Highlights

Discover these gems released between Nov 16, 2025 - Dec 15, 2025, already making waves in Early Access.

Scrabdackle

Moonlighter 2: The Endless Vault

The beloved shopkeeping ARPG returns. Delve into dungeons, haul back relics, and turn your humble storefront into the heart of a thriving village—every run fueling both your profits and Tresna’s revival.

$29.99 | PC

Cleared Hot

A physics-driven helicopter shooter where you haul gear, redirect missiles, sling enemies around, and ferry your squad into chaotic skirmishes across desert, jungle, and arctic battlefields.

$14.99 | PC

AFTERBLAST

Descend into the realm of the Evil Gods in this fast-paced action roguelite, where immortality is both your blessing and your curse. Hunt colossal bosses, wield magic-infused weapons, and chain-hook your way through swarms of enemies solo or in co-op.

$14.99 | PC

Umigame

A fast-paced roguelite where a battle-worn sea turtle wages war against a swamp witch’s curse. Harness the unstable power of the Omnicore, summon nature’s fury, and fight to reclaim a world on the brink of ruin.

$14.99 | PC

Scrabdackle

Cast from the academy, you tumble into a scribbly, hand-drawn wilderness that defies every textbook. Master new spells to unlock the path home, battling punishing bosses in a non-linear world teeming with secrets.

$14.99 | PC, Mac, Linux

Cloudheim

Ragnarok shattered the world, and the gods have chosen you to shape the next one. Cloudheim is a 1-4 player action adventure that blends crafting with chaotic physics-based combat. Awaken forgotten deities, drive back corruption, and upgrade your shop in the sky to bring harmony to a broken realm.

$29.99 | PC
