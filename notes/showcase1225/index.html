Showcase: Dec 25

From kickflipping through the underworld to managing a maple syrup racing empire, this collection thrives on distinct, high-concept hooks. Whether you are defibrillating hearts to a complex beat or surviving an 80s sci-fi nightmare, these titles demand mastery of unique mechanics across wildly different worlds.

Here’s just a handful of the wonderful releases you shouldn’t miss:

Death Howl

Skate Story

A fragile demon on a board, pushing through the nine layers of the Underworld with nothing but glass bones and a stubborn kickflip.

$19.99 | PC, Mac

Mon Bazou

Experience the golden age of tuners in a survival sim where maple syrup and cannabis sales fund your street racing career. Transform a rusty beater into a high-performance machine by manually wrenching every bolt, balancing your legitimate business with the underground thrill of the track.

$15.99 | PC

ROUTINE

Explore an eerie lunar base frozen in an 80s vision of the future, where silence hides something far worse. Search for answers through derelict corridors and flickering terminals, using your Cosmonaut Assistance Tool to survive whatever stalks the shadows.

$24.99 | PC, Xbox

Rhythm Doctor

Slam the spacebar on the seventh beat to defibrillate hearts in a hospital governed by music theory. Master complex polyrhythms and irregular signatures to save patients whose lives hang on your perfect timing.

$19.99 | PC, Mac, Linux

Death Howl

A grief-stricken hunter steps into the spirit realm to bring her son back, battling restless echoes in tight, turn-based encounters. Gather ingredients, craft powerful cards, and shape your deck with shamanic totems as you move through eerie forests, fractured biomes, and forgotten myths.

$19.99 | PC, PlayStation, Xbox

Outhold

A run-based loop built for “one more go.” Push as far as you can, bank your resources, then dive back in stronger. Build wild loadouts through a deep, fully refundable upgrade tree, rewarding experimentation and weird combinations.

$6.99 | PC, Mac

✦ NeverAwake FLASHBACK

A nightmare reborn as pure arcade mayhem. Battle through shifting stages in a fast-paced roguelite shooter where every run reshuffles your weapons, upgrades, and enemies.

$9.99 | PC

Light Up The Town

A blizzard looms over a darkened town, leaving the festival’s fate in the paws of a determined ferret. Cast shimmering lights across snowy rooftops to banish the cold and restore warmth to the community.

$14.99 | PC

✦ Villainy

A tight, back-stabby social deduction scramble where 4–10 players bluff, scheme, and panic their way through every match. Heroes race to finish objectives and survive escalating curses, while hidden Villains stalk the group, grab weapons, and slowly turn teamwork into chaos.

$7.99 | PC
