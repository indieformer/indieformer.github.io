Wield figurines and 17th-century incantations to purge a family curse in this Arctic card battler. Use a unique "Reserve System" to stack units and bargain with demons for the souls of the living.

Territory Control 2

Industrialize a 2D physics sandbox where 4X strategy meets ruthless exploitation. Siege enemy trenches with porridge-bound masonry and capybara-mounted machine guns to fuel your flimsy, mass-produced empire.

Genome Guardian 2

Build biomechanical death machines to purge a bioluminescent world. Fuse DNA nucleotides to discover 70 hybrid bioweapons, from stationary turrets to psychic motorbikes, backed by a bass-heavy soundtrack.

Humanica

Guide a civilization from the Paleolithic to the Iron Age in this turn-based hexagonal builder. Manage a strict daily action cycle to feed settlers, edit the literal landscape, and defend against nomadic raids.
