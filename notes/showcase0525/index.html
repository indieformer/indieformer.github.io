This Month’s Top Picks

Carefully curated indie releases that deserve your attention from May, 2025.

Rift Riff

despelote

A coming-of-age tale through the lens of football. Explore the streets of Quito as a young kid with a ball and a dream, where every kick unearths a story and the city hums with pre-World Cup tension.

Kabuto Park

A cosy bug-collecting battler where kids raise beetles and clash in tiny sumo duels. Catch critters, build decks, and live out the childhood summer you wish you had—no spiders, just vibes.

$4.99 | PC, Mac

Tainted Grail: The Fall of Avalon

A dark fantasy RPG where Arthurian legend has curdled into chaos. Shape your legacy across a sprawling open world filled with bleak quests, flexible builds, and choices that won’t let you off easy.

$44.99 | PC, PlayStation, Xbox

Oddsparks: An Automation Adventure

Automation, but adorable. Send your quirky companions on fetch quests, craft networks without conveyor belts, and build a base that grows with every Spark you send out into the world.

$29.99 | PC, PlayStation, Xbox

Rift Riff

Tower defense, but gentler. Schedule your builds, undo mistakes, and retry the last wave without penalty. Strategy still matters—but it doesn’t punish you for learning on the job.

Aureole - Wings of Hope

A speed-based platformer where halos fly and demons fall. Dash through gravity-defying levels with tight controls, chase leaderboard glory, and try to keep up with the beat.

$12.99 | PC, Switch, PlayStation, Xbox

Bahamut and the Waqwaq Tree

A mythic underwater journey where glowing light fends off creeping darkness. Swim through sunken ruins, interpret ancient texts, and uncover the secrets of a kingdom swallowed by the sea.

$14.99 | PC

The Precinct

A neon-noir cop sim soaked in 1980s grit. Patrol a living city full of petty crimes and major conspiracies, chasing suspects through alleyways, rainstorms, and high-speed pursuits—by car, foot, or chopper.

$29.99 | PC, PlayStation, Xbox

Of Life and Land

A meditative city builder where nature isn’t background—it’s a living force with needs, instincts, and consequences. Balance progress with preservation, or watch the wild push back.

$24.99 | PC

Preserve

Build vertical biomes tile by tile, where each plant and animal depends on the last. Create thriving ecosystems, solve subtle puzzles, and snap serene shots of the nature you’ve restored.

$12.49 | PC, Mac

The Deadseat

$2.99 | PC

Monster Train 2

Kathy Rain 2: Soothsayer
