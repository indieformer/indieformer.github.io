Introducing Patch Notes – our first ever game jam

A 72-hour sprint for broken ideas, weird experiments, and half-baked brilliance.

special·Issue #36 20 Jul 2025

Issue #36

PATCH NOTES

This isn’t a normal Indieformer email.

There are no game picks this time. No wrap-up. No deals.

Instead, we’re doing something new:

We’re hosting a game jam.

It’s called Patch Notes — a 72-hour jam for broken ideas, wild experiments, and the prototypes that never made it past the group chat.

Not perfect. Not polished. Just playable… Maybe.

🛠️ 72 hours to make something chaotic

📅 Sept 12 – 14

🎭 Theme revealed when the jam starts

🧪 Open to solo devs, teams, artists, writers, vibe curators — everyone

Winning games may be featured in a special newsletter, future interviews, or something even bigger.

This won’t be for everyone on this list — and that’s okay. But if you’re curious about making something messy (or just want to lurk and see what shows up), we’d love to have you.

WHY WE’RE DOING THIS

Indieformer exists to spotlight what’s next.

And sometimes, what’s next needs a little chaos to get going.

Let’s see what happens.
