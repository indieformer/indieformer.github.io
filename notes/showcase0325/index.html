SHOWCASE

This Month’s Top Picks

Carefully curated indie releases that deserve your attention from March, 2025.

Reignbreaker

Split Fiction

A boundary-pushing co-op action-adventure from the creators of It Takes Two. Writers Mio and Zoe are trapped in their own stories, switching between sci-fi and fantasy worlds. Tame dragons, fight cyber ninjas, and tackle wild challenges in this unpredictable journey of friendship.

$49.99 | PC, PlayStation, Xbox (Click this box for Steam)

Wanderstop

From the creators of The Stanley Parable, Gone Home, and Minecraft, step into the shoes of Alta, a warrior-turned-tea shopkeeper. Tend plants, brew tea, and serve travellers in a magical forest — though Alta’s reluctance to embrace this new life lingers.

$24.99 | PC, PlayStation, Xbox

↓ HIDDEN GEM ↓

Oblin Party

A chaotic 2D party brawler where you control two hands to swing, slingshot, and parkour through dynamic maps. Battle friends locally or online, or test your skills in solo time trials.

$7.99 | PC

Twilight Monk

A hand-drawn 2.5D action RPG combining metroidvania exploration and classic JRPG elements. Navigate dungeons, uncover secrets, and wield the Phantom Pillar in a dark, richly crafted world.

$19.99 | PC, Switch

↓ SUPPORTER’S CHOICE ↓

Rosewater

A beautifully crafted Western road trip adventure that captures the joy of storytelling. With memorable companions, meaningful choices, and stunning voice acting, it’s a journey that lingers long after the credits roll.

$19.99 | PC, Mac

↓ HIDDEN GEM ↓

Ligo

A refined Sokoban puzzle adventure with 200+ clever levels that push you to think outside the box. Its non-linear design keeps things fresh, offering a rewarding challenge for puzzle fans.

$14.99 | PC

Look Outside

A claustrophobic survival horror RPG where every trip outside your apartment risks monstrous encounters. Manage resources, forge alliances, and survive the grotesque unknown.

$9.99 | PC

Reignbreaker

A high-octane action roguelike set in a medievalpunk world. Play as Clef, a rebel storming the Queen’s fortress, clashing against high-tech machines and navigating traps. Motorized javelins. Explosive combat. Rebellion-fueled battle for freedom.

$9.99 | PC

↓ HIDDEN GEM ↓

Muffles' Life Sentence

In a prison where inmates are reshaped to reflect their crimes, swap faces to unlock new abilities and uncover the truth behind your sentence. With deckbuilding combat, bizarre characters, and dark humour, this episodic RPG offers a twisted journey of identity and rebellion.

Free | PC

ANEURISM IV

In a dystopian city consumed by decay, you exist as a Spirit on the edge of rebirth. Align with factions to protect, corrupt, or simply survive as the spreading Rot changes the world around you. No goals, no rules — just consequences.

$9.99 | PC, Mac

↓ HIDDEN GEM ↓

Wednesdays

Tim's memories resurface while revisiting a childhood video game, confronting the trauma of sexual abuse. Through fragmented storytelling, step into the perspectives of his family to piece together the past.

$9.99 | PC, Mac

Mudborne: Frog Management Sim

Awaken as a frog in a vibrant, abandoned pond and restore its lost ecosystem. Breed and genetically modify frogs, solve puzzles by hopping between the waking and dreaming worlds, and uncover what caused the disappearance of your amphibian friends.

$7.99 | PC, Mac
