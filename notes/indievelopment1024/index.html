The Spell Brigade | 16th Sept 2024

Steam • Trailer • $9.99

Team up with friends in this chaotic co-op bullet hell for 1-4 players, where friendly fire adds to the madness—though an off mode is on the way. Battle through hordes of monsters, complete team-based objectives, and unlock powerful spell synergies. With a need for more wizard diversity and biome-specific enemies, the action remains fast-paced and exciting.

Customize your wizard with spells, enchantments, and relics as you dive into survivor-style co-op chaos, where every match is a balance of teamwork and pure, unhinged fun.

Witchfire | 24th Sept 2024

Steam • Trailer • $39.99

Step into the role of an undead preyer in this intense single-player RPG shooter. Explore vast biomes, scavenge resources, and face powerful Familiars as you prepare to take down the Witch of the Black Sea. With tight, satisfying gunplay and spellcasting, the game offers a thrilling mix of challenge and strategy. Customise your preyer with unique weapons, spells, and artifacts, and shape your journey through the roguelite Arcana system.

Players are already hooked on the hidden items, the dynamic combat, and the immersive music—every detail pulls you deeper into this dark, witchfire-fueled world.

Cyberwar: Neon City | 24th Sept 2024

Steam • Trailer • $10.99

In a world overrun by cybernetic monkeys, you play as Printer, a clone who must fight alongside his loyal shark weapon, Blue, in a neon-lit dystopia. Face waves of enemies—monkeys, robots, and genetically modified creatures—in frenzied combat, unlocking new abilities, upgrades, and allies with each run. As you dive deeper into the tower, uncover a complex story filled with twists, memories, and mysterious characters.

With fast-paced rogue-like action and stunning pixel art, every attempt reveals new surprises as you grow stronger and push back against the cybernetic forces.

Silence of the Siren | 30th Sept 2024

Steam • Trailer • $29.99

A turn-based strategy game that blends the DNA of Heroes of Might and Magic III with modern mechanics and features.

Set in a distant star system cut off from the galaxy, you’ll explore planets, gather resources, and build powerful armies. As different factions vie for control, recruit commanders, level up their skills, and crush opponents in tactical battles.

Fans of classic strategy will love the blend of familiar mechanics with updated controls, but beware—the game’s powerful artifacts and units can quickly turn the tide of battle, making for an exciting, if occasionally unbalanced, experience.

Liar’s Bar | 3rd Oct 2024

Steam • Trailer • $6.99

Step into Liar’s Bar, the ultimate multiplayer deception game, perfect for sharing some chaotic fun with friends. With two modes available, you’ll experience the tension of calling out your opponents while risking it all with Russian Roulette.

Smooth mechanics, character animations, and voice chat add to the hilarious moments as you nod or shake your head to challenge lies. Best enjoyed with friends, this twisted bar promises even more thrilling game modes and updates in Early Access.

The Last Plague: Blight | 4th Oct 2024

Steam • Trailer • $16.99

In The Last Plague: Blight, every action—from boiling water to crafting tools—demands your full attention as the immersive mechanics pull you in for hours at a time. With a procedurally generated world that’s relentless in its challenges, you’ll face the harsh elements, wild beasts, and the constant need to tend to your well-being.

The game’s depth and relentless challenges make it one of the most realistic and addictive survival experiences out there. A must-have if you’re a fan of the survival genre.

Monomyth | 4th Oct 2024

Steam • Trailer • $19.99

If you’re craving an old-school first-person dungeon crawler with the depth of classic fantasy RPGs, MONOMYTH scratches that itch and more. From the atmospheric exploration to its non-linear story, the game pulls you in from the start. The intricate level design of Lysandria’s labyrinthine corridors will have you lost in the best way possible, while the combat—whether through melee, magic, or stealth—feels fluid and immersive, despite room for polish.

The challenge lies in its lack of hand-holding; quests require you to think, explore, and make use of your own notes. For fans of classic RPG dungeon crawlers, MONOMYTH offers a journey back to the golden age of the genre.

Tower of Dreams | 9th Oct 2024

Steam • Trailer • $11.99

Ascend the tower in this vertical platformer roguelike as the Dream Knight, using your sword to pogo off enemies and obstacles. With tight, challenging platforming, each run offers a unique experience thanks to random selections of handcrafted levels, items, and side areas.

Discover powerful upgrades and unlock cosmetics as you climb through three tower floors. Smooth controls, stunning visuals, and replayability, with every restart feeling fresh and rewarding.
