Indievelopment: June’s Early Access

Discover tomorrow's indie hits, today.

indievelopment·Issue #01 22 Jun 2024

Pilot Issue #01

For the best experience, enjoy this newsletter on a desktop with a warm cup of coffee ☕️.

Welcome to the first edition of Indievelopment!

Gaming is becoming more accessible and exciting every week, and we're here to give you a glimpse into its future.

Early Access is better than ever, offering the perfect opportunity to snag amazing games at lower prices before their full release. It’s an exciting time to be a gamer, with fresh innovations and stunning titles constantly emerging.

All price references below are in USD.

Let’s dive in.

8th June 2024

Steam (Free) • Trailer • Website

Dive into a hardcore fantasy FPS dungeon PvPvE adventure! Team up with friends to uncover mythical treasures, defeat gruesome monsters, and outsmart rival treasure-hunters.

Upgrade in-game for about $30.

8th June 2024

Steam ($33.32) • Trailer • Website

“The first ultra-realistic multiplayer FPS” game using Unreal Engine 5, delivering intense, fast-paced action where every choice counts.

This instant classic, developed by two young French enthusiasts, pushes the boundaries of photorealism and realism, offering a revolutionary experience in the genre.

15th June 2024

Steam ($18.95) • Trailer • Twitter

The A.C.E.S, guardians of Switchblade City, are being hunted down. One man with two fists must uncover the truth. A crime noir FPS with a '90s vibe and comic book style.

Spanning three episodes, the first is out now, featuring 100% original hand-drawn art and animation, offering a unique and nostalgic gaming experience.

20th June 2024

Steam ($19.99) • Trailer • Website

Dive into a roguelike turn-based exploration game where you build your team, brave hostile terrains, and seek to resurrect the Mother Tree Umama.

Goblinz Studio from France packs a punch with its charming pixel art graphics. Turn-based action never gets old!

21st June 2024

Steam ($24.99) • Trailer • Website

Claim new lands and lead a fierce Viking tribe in an epic survival journey. Build the ultimate settlement solo or with up to three friends, summoning intelligent NPC villagers for support.

Prepare to build more than a shelter—you're creating a village. Dive into a colony sim where every decision shapes your thriving community.

Thanks for diving into this month's in-development indie games with us. From thrilling adventures to nostalgic graphics, we’ve peeked into the innovative titles and passionate developers set to define the gaming landscape.

Until then, happy gaming!

The Indieformer Team
