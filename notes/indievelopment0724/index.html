Indievelopment: July's Games-in-Creation

There's a bit of Studio Ghibli, gardening, and a Raccoon coming soon from devs around the world.

indievelopment·Issue #03 20 Jul 2024

Pilot Issue #03

For the best experience, enjoy this newsletter on a desktop with a warm cup of coffee ☕️.

We’ve got Early Access on the tables, but just before we get into those, we thought perhaps some noteworthy crowdfunding projects were needed.

Click the thumbnails in our main section to visit the developer website. If there are no prices, the games are being released after this edition was published.

All price references below are in USD.

Let’s dive in.

Crowdfunding

  • From a modest goal of $15,000 to a staggering $140,000, this brand-new SHMUP (Shoot ‘em up) for the SEGA Mega Drive & Genesis offers classic horizontal shooter action. ZPF

  • Fully funded and set for a 2025 release on PC and consoles, this 3-player couch co-op is a SNES-styled Action Adventure RPG inspired by Dark Cloud, Final Fantasy XI, and the Mana series. Infinity Knights: Xross

  • Turn a rundown intergalactic hotel into a thriving tourist destination in this cozy management sim inspired by Studio Ghibli. Nostalgia and fairy-tale magic await. Hotel Galactic

  • With every single detail hand drawn, it looks like a picture book come to life. Set in a fictional 19th century, Play as Veteran Nico who takes up writing after the war. Sunset Hills

  • Relax with this 90s-styled toy where you garden at your own pace. Plant seeds, turn the crank, and watch everything grow. No pressure, no score, just pure chill. Tiny Garden

  • Meet Tofu, a small mouse in a big world! Dive into pixel art platforming in this new Metroidvania destined to be an instant classic. Pipsqueak!

  • Enter a dark fantasy Edo Japan in this unapologetic, one-shot sidescroller. Slice through the Army of One-thousand in this instant-death, one-hit-kill adventure. One-Thousand Cuts

  • Experience a thrilling third-person adventure in a rich, explorable world. Scavenge, survive, uncover secrets, and restore what's lost in this standalone epic from KeokeN Interactive, the third crowdfunded piece in their acclaimed series! Deliver Us Home

  • Funded by the skin of its teeth, this atmospheric action-adventure lets you morph between human, raccoon, raven, and wolf. Explore lush meadows, solve puzzles, and engage in combat. Edenfall: Legacy Of The First Wardens

6th July 2024

Steam ($3.99) • Trailer

Math… but fun? SVGames has turned the mundane calculator into an exciting roguelike adventure! Each calculator you collect brings a unique strategy and play style, transforming a boring device into an engaging tactical experience.

Dive into and see how math becomes a thrilling challenge.

9th July 2024

Steam ($21.99) • Trailer

Build a mobile fortress on the back of a giant, mysterious walking tree and embark on an epic journey. This game blends tower defense with colony simulation, featuring a deep tech tree.

It's in Early Access, so some polish is still needed. If you love Oxygen Not Included, you'll enjoy this – offering 20-30 hours of playtime.

16th July 2024

Steam ($12.99) • Trailer

Galactic Glitch is a roguelike shooter with a twist: physics! Hurl rocks, snatch missiles out of the air, and dismantle enemies to repurpose as ammo. Customise your builds from a diverse arsenal of weapons and powers.

The animations are incredibly smooth, and the vibrant colors truly stand out.

19th July 2024

Steam ($9.99) • Trailer

Old School Rally merges retro charm with the thrill of international rally racing. This fast-paced, arcade-style game demands driving skill with high-speed maneuvers. Race across global tracks on dirt, tarmac, and snow, and challenge your driving skills.

Its visuals reminiscent of '90s rally games, OSR is a nostalgic must-play for only 10 bucks.

22nd July 2024

Steam • Trailer

Brick by brick, design and build fortresses to withstand endless Horrors in this intense real-time strategy game. Master resource management, siege defense, and exploration. Lead from the ramparts, push back the darkness, and face the creatures of the Mist.

From the creators of Moonlighter, a classic in-the-making.

23rd July 2024

Steam ($19.99) • Trailer

In VORAX, survival isn't just a game; it's a harrowing journey. Dive into this Open World Survival Horror and prepare for a relentless battle where every decision, every step, and every breath could be your last.

IndieGala hit the stage with their 10th game.

25th July 2024

Steam • Trailer

It's the end of days for this fringe terraforming colony, and you're the last line of defense. Relentless Frontier is a fast-paced sci-fi FPS on the GZDoom Engine, emphasising mobility, strategy, and exploration.

Play as Noah Gansky, a combat engineer armed with his wits, agility, and Omniaxe. Battle alien bugs, energy cultists, and super-soldiers to save Ross 128-b and escape the Solar Dominion's grasp.

25th July 2024

Steam • Trailer

Congratulations, you’ve bought your own planet! This agrarian gem at the galaxy's edge was practically a giveaway. It's time to swap your hectic, stressful city life for a tranquil, rewarding rural existence.

July 2024

Steam • Trailer

Full throttle retro racer! Zoom through iconic tracks in London, Tokyo, Rome, USA, The Alps, and more. Unlock 48 exciting courses, choose your team and car, and jump straight into the action.

Relive the 90s arcade racing boom with modern graphics and a custom soundtrack. Compete globally on online leaderboards or race a friend in split-screen mode.

2nd Aug 2024

Steam • Trailer

Tomb Explorer VR is an adventure game where you explore ancient ruins and tombs for historical treasures and artifacts. Combining action and puzzles, this VR experience echoes the thrill of Indiana Jones and Tomb Raider.

Navigate vast worlds like Petra Tombs, Inca Jungles, and Egyptian Tombs to uncover past mysteries.

Our Mates

A small section of articles from our internet neighbours.

  • Looking for a puzzle game to play? Demo Disk reviewed the demo of A Bonnie Odyssey. Explore an enigmatic, abandoned island in a retrofuturist puzzle mystery. Set in the wilds of Scotland, this open-world sci-fi adventure follows a father and daughter separated by time.

  • Getting ahead of our next edition, Tiny Gaming reviewed Flock. “A casual, gentle collectathon where you and your loyal bird glide around a colorful, tranquil landscape in search of goobers.”

  • Our mates across the country in Adelaide, The IndiEXP, posted a Press Release about upcoming indie game Wren’s Resurgence. A thrilling 2D action platformer by solo developer Sam Leitch, follows Wren's epic quest to rescue her sister from Yokai-infested lands and restore peace with her katana.
