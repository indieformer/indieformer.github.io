Early Access Highlights

Discover these gems released between Nov 18 - Dec 10, 2024, already making waves in Early Access.

RAM: Random Access Mayhem

A reinvention of the top-down roguelike shooter by letting you play as the enemies you fight. As a bodiless AI, you'll strategically swap between eight unique enemy types, mastering their movesets to unleash stylish carnage across procedurally generated ruins of a world long abandoned by humans.

$4.99 (Click this box for Steam)

Dimensionals

Dive into an exhilarating roguelike RPG adventure with a vast array of heroes to collect and elevate, where strategic mastery and character evolution are key. Experience the thrill of ever-changing challenges and dynamic gameplay that keeps you coming back for more.

$19.99

Path of Exile 2

Dive into a sprawling, dark world where every decision shapes your survival. With six-player co-op, customizable builds, and over 100 unique bosses, the battle against creeping Corruption has never felt more intense.

$29.99

Dungeon Clawler

Enter a unique roguelike world where your deck-building prowess meets claw machine mechanics in a quest for the ultimate arsenal. Strategize your way through relentless dungeons, grabbing crucial weapons and items with every pull.

$9.99

Midori no Kaori

Immerse yourself in a serene world where you can create and manage your own Japanese-themed shop, balancing business with relaxation. Revel in the calming gameplay that blends creativity with cultural beauty.

$9.99

Beat the Humans

Command a formidable alien army in this turn-based tactical strategy game, where Earth's fate hangs in the balance. Outsmart and outmaneuver human defenses in a battle for planetary dominance.

$4.99

Wild Woods

Team up as fearless feline adventurers, navigating a perilous forest by day and fending off bandit critters by night. Gather resources, defend your wagon, and balance risk with reward in this charming, chaotic co-op journey.

$14.99

Gladio Mori

Step into an arena of chaotic fun with physics-driven gladiator battles and epic slow-motion combat moments that defy gravity. This multiplayer spectacle invites you to become the master of the ring—or fall trying.

$15.99

New Arc Line

Navigate the clash of mystical arcane forces against the relentless steampunk revolution, where your decisions could tip the scales of a world on the brink. The storyline unfolds in a richly detailed setting of magic and machinery.

$19.99

Guidus Zero

Embark on a real-time, grid-based roguelike journey through a mysterious sinkhole, challenging your tactical skills at every turn. Discover hidden secrets and unfathomable depths in an ever-evolving subterranean world.

$14.99
