This Month’s Top Picks

Carefully curated indie releases that deserve your attention from June, 2025.

PEAK

DELTARUNE

A chaotic, charming follow-up to a beloved cult classic. Dodge bullets, befriend weirdos, and try not to break your bond with the only person who knows you're controlling them.

$24.99 | PC, Mac, Switch, PlayStation

Cyber Knights: Flashpoint

Gridless stealth, tactical shootouts, and AI that doesn’t wait its turn. In a world of collapsing systems and corporate dominance, build your crew, plan your heist, and survive the fallout.

$29.99 | PC, Mac

✦ Zefyr: A Thief's Melody

Sail the seas on a turtle, sneak through sunlit islands, and face towering guardians in this wholesome open-world adventure where kindness counts as much as cleverness.

$19.99 | PC

Backpack Battles

Pack smarter, fight harder. A PvP autobattler where victory hinges on item placement, synergy stacking, and one extremely strategic backpack.

$14.99 | PC

Idle Waters

An idle fishing sim that runs quietly in the corner, catching you loot while you work. Quest, collect, and build aquariums without ever breaking a sweat.

$4.99 | PC

✦ Through the Nightmares

A nightmare platformer with rhythm-timed traps, shape-shifting movement, and surreal dream logic. Escape the god of sleep and free the children trapped in his twisted world.

$19.99 | PC, Switch, Xbox, PlayStation

PEAK

Rope up with friends or die alone. Scale a deadly mountain one ledge at a time in this punishing co-op climb built around trust, grit, and questionable snacks.

$7.99 | PC

Tower Wizard

Summon spirits, gather magic, and click your way to the top of a tower that never builds itself. Chill-paced, elegantly crafted, and perfect for idle game enjoyers.

$2.99 | PC

✦ 10S FOREVER

Tennis, but hellish. 8 stages, 1 champion, and one very cursed backhand. Difficult, stylish, and emotionally suspicious.

$7.99 | PC

Cast n Chill

A pixel-perfect fishing sim that’s as relaxing as it is addictive. Pet your dog, reel in legendary catches, and unlock new lakes until the world disappears and it’s just you and the water.

$14.99 | PC, Mac

✦ Ruffy and the Riverside

Reality’s flexible when you’re the Chosen One. Transform the world mid-puzzle, leap between 3D and 2D, and try not to let the charm distract you from the trickier bits.

$19.99 | PC, Switch, PlayStation, Xbox

Suit for Hire

Slide, shoot, and smash your way through stylised assassinations in this top-down (or third-person) action blast of martial arts mayhem and high-calibre chaos. BIG John Wick energy.

$14.99 | PC

✦ Blessed Burden on Steam

Descend into the inferno as the last priest of a godless world. This fast-paced platformer mixes speedrun bait with infernal architecture and a choir-backed soundtrack that slaps.

$6.99 | PC
