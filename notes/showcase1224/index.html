This Month’s Top Picks

Carefully curated indie releases that deserve your attention from December, 2024.

The Edge of Allegoria

Dive headfirst into a retro-styled adventure brimming with sharp wit and unfiltered chaos. Master over 80 skills and crush 144 unique enemies in a world of madness, mystery, and mayhem.

$19.99 | PC (Click this box for Steam)

Caves of Qud

Carve through ancient civilizations and shape a living, unpredictable world where every choice spawns chaos or discovery. Mutate, explore, and survive in a bizarre tapestry of science fantasy.

$29.99 | PC, Mac

Awaria

Survive deadly hazards, repair failing generators, and try not to crack under relentless pressure in a claustrophobic world of chaos. It’s punishing, unpredictable, and surprisingly thrilling—just don’t forget to kiss the ghost.

Free | PC, Android

HIDDEN GEM

Weirdlands

Locked in your room, a portal opens to a magical land where spirits await to be captured, fused, and unleashed. Solve puzzles your way, uncover layers of lore, and experience a second playthrough that flips the game on its head.

$19.99 | PC

ANTONBLAST

Smash through chaotic, destructible worlds with a hammer in hand, chasing down stolen spirits and setting detonators before time runs out. It's fast, furious, and gloriously over-the-top—a true power trip of platforming madness.

$19.99 | PC, Switch

Diesel Legacy: The Brazen Age

Step into chaotic 2v2 brawls where strategy, teamwork, and a touch of madness collide. With lane-switching mayhem, ghost comebacks, and a dieselpunk twist, this is a fighting game battlefield like no other.

$29.99 | PC, Switch, Xbox, PlayStation

HIDDEN GEM

NAIAD

Drift through serene waters, befriending animals and uncovering secrets in a vibrant, hand-crafted river world. Swim, dive, and dash your way through a journey of growth, beauty, and quiet resistance against those who threaten nature.

$19.99 | PC, Mac, Xbox, PlayStation

Janosik 2

Embark on a daring rescue with a highlander, a werewolf, and a vampire as you battle mythical foes and unravel sinister plots. With diverse abilities, sprawling levels, and multiple endings, this adventure promises chaos, camaraderie, and maybe even a mountable sheep.

$8.99 | PC

Slopecrashers

Hit the slopes with a chaotic crew of animal riders, pulling off wild stunts and chaining combos across snow, sand, and cyberpunk skylines. Whether it’s split-screen mayhem or online showdowns, this is snowboarding reimagined—jetpacks, frying pans, and all.

$17.99 | PC, Switch, Xbox

Symphonia

Revive a fading world through music as a mysterious violinist, using your bow for both melodies and movement. Solve platforming challenges, gather prodigious musicians, and bring life back to a realm where silence looms.

$19.99 | PC, Xbox, PlayStation

Endless Dark

Endure isolation and unravel the mysteries of your existence as a sentient machine tasked with preserving a crumbling sleeper ship. With no enemies but time and your own unraveling sanity, every decision shapes a haunting narrative of purpose and decay.

$7.99 | PC
