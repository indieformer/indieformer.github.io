SHOWCASE

September 2025 Highlights

This is the first Showcase since Waypoint went live — which means these picks are now highlights from a much bigger pool. Over September we added:

  • 15 Showcase games

  • ✦ 9 Hidden Gems

  • 11 extras

Here’s just a handful of the wonderful releases you shouldn’t miss:

CloverPit

✦ Henry Halfhead

Slip into the life of Henry, a peculiar little being who can become over 250 different objects—from toast-slicing knives to paper planes.

$12.99 | PC, Switch, PlayStation

Baby Steps

Wobble through life as Nate, an unemployed failson who discovers his one true talent: walking. Step by awkward step, hike misty mountains, trip over rocks, soil your onesie, and maybe even find meaning along the way.

$19.99 | PC, PlayStation

Hades II

Supergiant’s first sequel expands the god-like roguelike with witchcraft, Olympian boons, and a deeper Underworld teeming with gods, ghosts, and foes.

$29.99 | PC, Mac, Switch

✦ Heroes of the Seven Islands

Mix point-and-click exploration with fast, turn-based combat, hundreds of scrappy illustrations, and a prophecy that may—or may not—be worth believing.

$11.99 | PC, Mac, Linux

Megabonk

Survive waves of enemies and bosses across shifting maps, grabbing loot and stacking upgrades to push your run further.

$9.99 | PC, Linux

Hollow Knight: Silksong

Leap, slash, and craft tools to conquer 200+ foes and dozens of towering bosses in a vast new adventure from the world of Hollow Knight.

$19.99 | Pc, Mac, Linux, Switch, Xbox, PlayStation

✦ Auridia

A short metroidvania built on fluid platforming, where grapples, wall-bounces, and clever chains of movement reveal hidden paths, artifacts, and the lost history of a fallen civilisation.

$9.99 | PC, Mac

CloverPit

A roguelite of luck, debt, and desperation. Trapped in a cell with nothing but a cursed slot machine, you’ll gamble for coins, stack broken synergies, and try to outlast your captor long enough to escape hell itself.

$9.99 | PC

RAM: Random Access Mayhem

As a bodiless AI, you'll strategically swap between eight unique enemy types, mastering their movesets to unleash stylish carnage across procedurally generated ruins of a world long abandoned by humans.

$14.99 | PC, Mac

✦ Mole Maiden

Switch between precise platforming and 360° drilling, face eccentric bosses, and meet a colourful cast in a heartfelt action-adventure about grit and growth.

$15.99 | PC, Mac

Strange Antiquities

Step into a candlelit shop of occult curiosities, where every object hides a mystery and every choice has consequences.

$17.99 | PC, Switch

✦ PANIK

Guide quirky, terrified little creatures through over 100 tricky puzzles across 8 worlds. Each paniker comes with their own oddball abilities—a ninja, a robot, even one wearing a crown—and it’s up to you to keep them calm long enough to reach safety.

$9.99 | PC

These highlights only scratch the surface. Waypoint has the complete calendar of September 2025’s indie releases, extras, and even more hidden gems.

Happy gaming!
