Goblin Cleanup

Take the mop after the heroes are gone. Scrub blood, reset traps, restock loot, and respawn monsters to keep dungeons ready for the next batch of adventurers.

PIGFACE

Wake up bleeding, a bomb drilled into your skull, and a phone demanding blood. In this brutal, fast-paced FPS, you’re blackmailed into taking contracts—clearing combat sandbox missions with an arsenal of hammers, rifles, mines, and more.

Ratatan

A rhythm roguelike where you command swarms of Ratatans to the beat, clashing in chaotic side-scrolling melees. Play solo or co-op, issue commands in time with the music, and march toward the Everafter in a vibrant 2D adventure.

$24.99 | PC

My Card Is Better Than Your Card!

Build your own cards with stickers in this charming roguelike deckbuilder bursting with childhood imagination. Mix and match over 200 stickers to craft unique cards, battle quirky neighborhood rivals, and uncover playful summer adventures.

$14.99 | PC

MADHOUSE

A blood-soaked action RPG with a chibi twist, this reimagined cult classic drips with early-2000s edge and 90s punk horror flair. Slash, shoot, and seethe your way through Grayville’s twisted streets on a vengeful hunt for your wife’s killer.
