Early Access Highlights

Discover these gems released between Aug 16, 2025 - Sep 15, 2025, already making waves in Early Access.

TurnBreak

Assemble and evolve a squad of oddball beasts, seal special triggers, and pull a clutch TurnBreak to end rounds on your terms. Face echo versions of past runs, gamble every floor, and even let Twitch chat meddle with your matches.

$7.99 | PC, Linux

Funguys Swarm

Hold the line against flaming hordes as silly forest weirdos, stacking mid-run upgrades into ridiculous power trips. Rescue critters to grow a Sanctuary for permanent boosts, unlock heroes, pets—even mechas.

$4.99 | PC

Star Birds

Build 360° factories on potato-shaped asteroids, automate production chains, and fulfill your space-birds’ odd requests. Scan, mine, and expand to new star systems in a cozy campaign from the Dorfromantik team and Kurzgesagt.

$19.99 | PC

Stick It to the Stickman

Climb the world’s worst corporate ladder with absurd weapons, side hustles, and a whole lot of window-kicking. Fight CEOs, crush quotas, and commit dubiously legal “growth” in co-op or solo chaos.

$4.99 | PC, Mac

Gunlocked 2

Pilot new ships and pilots with unique weapon masteries, equip augments, and stack infinite utilities for wild builds. Active dodge, “Air Brake,” 360° movement, and branching warp gates keep every run snappy and fresh.

$9.99 | PC

VOID/BREAKER

Blaze through an AI prison with ultrafast movement, on-the-fly gun modding, and fully destructible arenas. Every run reshuffles fights, routes, and risks in a roguelite FPS from the dev of The Entropy Centre.

$19.99 | PC, Xbox

DAVY x JONES

Command a headless pirate body while your wisecracking skull scouts the Locker’s sky-islands—sword, gun, grappling hook in hand. Fuse enemy essences into loot (and unleashed fiends), crew up on your living ship-whale, and hunt Blackbeard.

$9.99 | PC

Supraworld on Steam

A toy-box 3D platformer where every block is climbable, smashable, or suspicious. Explore, experiment, and push the edges of what the world will let you do.

$29.99 | PC

Eyes of Hellfire

Co-op gothic horror in a cursed Irish lodge where every player hides a curse and shifting motives. Manage hands of action cards, use proximity voice to scheme, and resist a reality-warping Host—Friend’s Pass lets pals try the first scenario.

$9.99 | PC

Burger Bots Inc.

Run the galaxy’s messiest burger joint in up to 8-player online co-op: assign roles, shout orders, and survive the rush. Clean the chaos, serve the weird, and keep rent paid—stranger things want fries with that.

$7.99 | PC
