February 2026 Highlights

Here’s just a handful of the wonderful releases you shouldn’t miss:

Hermit and Pig

✦ Gravethorn

Wield the weight of unfinished penance as an Inquisitor navigating a non-linear, rot-stricken world where the church and the land bleed together. Master deliberate, high-stakes combat to unearth fragmented secrets and decide how far you'll sink into the curse to understand it.

$4.99 | PC

The Dark Rites of Arkham

A pulp-inspired point-and-click mystery set in the shadowed streets of Arkham. Explore over 70 atmospheric pixel-art locations, interrogate suspects, and uncover a conspiracy tied to the witches of Salem and the end of the world itself.

$14.99 | PC

Insider Trading

Manipulate the stock market in a razor-sharp roguelike deckbuilder, playing cards to pump or crash prices to meet weekly financial targets. Draft risky assets in the Aftermarket and master unique trader quirks, balancing greed against volatility to amass a fortune without losing your real-life savings.

$12.89 | PC

✦ Tomb of the Bloodletter

Channel unpredictable magic through your keyboard in a strategic word roguelike where every letter is a weapon. Master tactical sequencing over vocabulary size, choosing from unique classes to navigate a haunted tomb while adapting to a chaotic, ever-changing deck of cursed letter-spells.

$7.99 | PC

D1AL-ogue

Master a circular diagnostic console to repair broken androids in a neon-drenched cyberpunk workshop. Rotate and merge internal modules within tight battery limits, purging electronic narcotics to mend the mechanical souls of Chroma City’s last outcasts.

Free | PC

Trust Me, I Nailed It

Fabricate a legendary hero in a timeline-based puzzle game where you edit battle footage for a hopeless warrior. Analyze enemy patterns to splice together a fake "flawless victory," applying VFX to transform clumsy moves into epic feats. Satisfy the dopamine-addicted Demon Lord and craft a false narrative to save the world within seven days.

Free | PC

✦ Hermit and Pig

Master fighting-game inputs to brawl through a conspiracy with your truffle-hunting pig, racing the clock in fast-paced turn-based combat. Navigate a world where social anxiety is lethal and every awkward conversation inflicts literal cringe damage..

$14.99 | PC, Mac

✦ Lovish

Abandon your allies in a fit of romantic jealousy, slashing through a devil’s castle to claim the princess for yourself. Survive bite-sized levels interspersed with chaotic, randomized events where anything from RPG battles to sudden apocalypses can strike.

$14.99 | PC, Switch, PlayStation, Xbox

Creature Kitchen

Sizzle, chop, and fry over 40 supernatural recipes in a "creepy-cozy" 2000s-style cooking sim. Explore a whispering forest to forage ingredients and feed the local cryptids, snapping photos of your monstrous friends before the witching hour ends.

$7.99 | PC

✦ Manairons

Awaken as a mischievous spirit in a 3D platformer inspired by Catalan folklore, fighting to save the Pyrenees from industrial greed. Wield a magical flute to command your kin, playing melodies to solve puzzles and battle the landowner exploiting your people for labor.

$24.99 | PC, PlayStation

These highlights only scratch the surface. Waypoint has the complete calendar of February 2026’s indie releases, extras, and even more hidden gems.
