The premise

You died. And you woke up here — in Abelina, alongside a few other after-lifers whose ideas about the universe have just been badly shaken. You're Vasily, and now you're trying to work out what this place actually is.

It's a mature, hand-crafted story about life and death, told through who you meet and what they tell you. That's as much as we'll say for now. We're still shaping it with Gabe, and we'll show more — the world, how it plays, the people in it — when it's ready.

Hand-crafted — by people who've done this at the top.

Abelina is built by hand, frame by frame. "Hand-crafted" here doesn't mean first-timer — it means a CG-animation veteran and a Webby-winning composer choosing to build every shot and every sound themselves.

The whole game

Gabe Askew

Solo developer · art, animation, design

A career in CG animation — nine years at Hornet directing campaigns for Dolby, Purina and Campbell's, then supervising cinematics at Blur Studio. Abelina is all of that pointed at one strange, personal world: hand-crafted, solo.
