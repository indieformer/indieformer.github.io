Press Kit Guide
for Indie Games.

Your press kit isn't for your players. It's for the people who decide whether to share your game — reviewers, YouTubers, curators, festival organisers, platform reps. They've got 60 seconds and 50 other emails. The kit is what makes those 60 seconds easy.

We're putting this together because the indie devs we love working with often don't have one ready when we ask. If your game has been out for two years and you still don't have a press kit, you're not behind. You're normal. Just start.

Pair this with the Steam Marketing Guide for the storefront side.

1
Factsheet / Overview the foundation
Include
  • Game Art (portrait / landscape)
  • About the Game (short + long)
  • Key Features (2–4 bullets)
  • Developer Name + Studio (if any)
  • Publisher (if applicable)
  • Genre & Steam Tags
  • Platforms (confirmed & planned)
  • Price (USD + major currencies)
  • Release Date or Estimate
  • Website + Socials
  • Press Contact Email

→ Export as PDF + .TXT and make downloadable.

Folder /presskit/ factsheet.pdf factsheet.txt
2
Videos & Clips let others show your game
Include
  • Trailers (.MP4, 1080p)
  • Short clips for social media (under 30s)
  • GIFs showing key gameplay or features
  • Optional: Raw gameplay footage (clean UI)
Folder /presskit/video/ trailer/ gifs/ broll/
3
Screenshots & Imagery high-res, ready to grab
Include
  • 6–10 screenshots (1920×1080+)
  • A mix of gameplay, combat, UI, and story
  • A few UI-free shots for flexibility
  • Concept art, character sheets, enemies
  • Optional: Developer photos (great for press)
Folder /presskit/images/ screenshots/ concept/ characters/
4
Logos, Branding & Graphic Assets flexible + share-ready
Include
  • Logo (full colour + mono, PNG/SVG)
  • Fonts (TTF or license links)
  • Steam capsules, banners, thumbnails
  • Key Art
  • Icons (e.g. square formats)
  • Heavily recommended: Working files (.AI, .PSD, or .SVG)
Folder /presskit/branding/ logos/ keyart/ fonts/
5
Press Releases past announcements + milestones
Include
  • All press releases in PDF or TXT
  • Group by year or milestone
  • Link any media coverage if relevant
Folder /presskit/ pressreleases/
6
Download Packs for the lazy reviewer (us)
Include
  • All assets zipped by type
  • A "complete press kit" zip
  • Optional: Separate packs for creators, press, publishers
Folder /presskit/downloads/ creator-pack/ press-kit.zip
