Description

One-liner

Feed the Scorchpot is a dice roguelike about feeding a dragon for twenty years — roll ingredients, build your island, and fuse absurd recipes into combos that break the score wide open. Miss a meal and you're next on the menu.

Short

You struck a deal with a dragon: twenty years of protection for twenty years of dinner service. Every year the dragon wakes hungrier. Roll dice to work your hex-grid island, harvest ingredients, fuse them into recipes, and cook them down in the Scorchpot — the pot you keep full or end up in.

Long

Feed the Scorchpot is a cozy-chaotic tabletop roguelike. Build farmsteads, shrines, and harbors across your island, each one changing how the board pays out, then fuse ingredients through the signature Fodder × Flavor × Essence system to multiply a handful of tiles into board-breaking numbers. The best runs end with a machine that buries the dragon in food — right before the odds tighten, the dragon turns picky, and you tear the whole thing down and rebuild while it's on fire. Cozy on top, ruthless underneath. And yes, you can pet the dragon.

Key Features

🏗️Build a scoring enginePlace farms, shrines and harbors so they feed each other. 50+ buildings and 80+ recipes, until a handful of tiles becomes board-breaking numbers.
🍲Every meal is a math puzzleFuse ingredients into recipes that boost Flavor and Essence, multiplying your Fodder score into absurd, screen-shaking totals.
🎲Build your dice20+ dice types: odd, even, colored, even dice carved into six sixes. Tune your tray and pray the dragon's merciful.
🔥Press your luckWin in fewer years for a bigger payoff — but each year raises the stakes: harsher odds, hungrier dragons, the odd tantrum that torches your board.
🧩Broken combos, on purposeBend the rules, break the math, and rebuild when everything catches fire.
✍️Hand-drawn, every inchEvery dragon, building and meal drawn by Daniela, the artist half of the married duo who made the game.

The Story

A 20-year deal with a dragon.

You struck a deal: twenty years of protection for twenty years of dinner service. The island thrives under the beast's watchful eye — as long as the meals keep coming. Every year it wakes up hungrier, pickier, and more dangerous. Get the meal right and you prosper. Get it wrong and you're smoke.

Feed the Scorchpot is the first game Moravian — Czech married duo Jakub and Daniela — have put out into the world, and the first Indieformer has put its name on as publisher. It sits comfortably alongside Balatro and Slay the Spire: a fresh spin on the roguelike where dice rolls fuel both chaos and creativity.

The Team

the developer

Moravian

The creative partnership of husband-and-wife team Jakub and Daniela, based in the Czech Republic. Jakub does design and code; Daniela hand-draws every dragon, building and meal. Feed the Scorchpot is their first commercial release.

the publisher

A small, creator-led publisher run by Josh & Clem in Melbourne, Australia. Twelve years writing about indie games; now we publish them — and write about that too, mistakes included.
